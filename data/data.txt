Razorpay is a fintech company founded in 2014 that provides digital payment solutions for businesses in India. It enables companies to accept, process, and disburse payments through its platform. Razorpay offers a payment gateway that supports multiple payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets. This allows businesses to accept payments seamlessly from customers. The platform also provides RazorpayX, a business banking solution that helps companies manage payouts, payroll, and vendor payments efficiently. Razorpay offers subscription-based billing, enabling businesses to automate recurring payments for services such as memberships and SaaS products. Razorpay provides APIs and developer tools that allow businesses to integrate payment solutions into their websites and applications easily. The company supports startups, small businesses, and large enterprises by offering scalable financial solutions and analytics to track transactions and performance. Razorpay runs a Startup Program that provides early-stage startups with discounted transaction fees, faster onboarding, and access to growth tools. The platform also offers Smart Collect, a feature that allows businesses to automatically reconcile incoming payments and track them in real time. Razorpay provides instant settlement options, enabling businesses to receive payments quickly instead of waiting for standard settlement cycles. The company occasionally offers promotional discounts on transaction fees for new merchants during onboarding. Security is a key focus for Razorpay. The platform uses encryption, tokenization, and compliance with PCI-DSS standards to ensure safe transactions. Customer support is available to assist users with payment issues, integration help, and account-related queries.
